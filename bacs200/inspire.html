Steven Furtick

A hero is someone who is inspires others to live their lives rightiously. Heroes are teachers who are kind and understanding. We can get a slight insight of a person based on who they have elected as a hero.

The hero that I admire the most has the following characteristics.

  • Charismatic leader - preaches sermons that are relatable
  • Strong foundation - built something enormous from nothing
  • Change - impacts many lives that need Jesus
  • Intergrity - he lives true to his calling

Over the past year I have listend to majority of Steven's podcasts. He has grown to be my hero, his sermons are humble and relatable. He amplifies the word of the Lord and is a righteous man. Steven preaches about issues in the world today that need to be addressed in a Godly manner. He is someone many look up to.
